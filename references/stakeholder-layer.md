# Stakeholder Communication Layer (English Edition)

Use this reference to present research in a way decision-makers can act on.

## Audience Mapping
For each audience, define:
- What they optimize for
- What risk they manage
- What decision they must make

## Narrative Structure (SCQA)
1. Situation
2. Complication
3. Question
4. Answer (with evidence)
5. Action owner + timeline

## Delivery Formats
- 1-page executive brief
- 10-slide insight deck
- Team walkthrough with Q&A

## Evidence Rules
- Every key claim must map to concrete evidence
- Distinguish validated finding vs hypothesis
- Include confidence level and limitations

## Objection Handling
Prepare counters for:
- "Sample is too small"
- "We already knew this"
- "This slows delivery"

## Adoption Checklist
- Link recommendation to existing KPIs
- Identify implementation owner
- Define first measurable outcome
- Set review date

## Output Contract
- Top findings (3)
- Recommended decisions (1–3)
- Trade-offs and risks
- Next actions with owners
