# Research Operations (English Edition)

Use this reference for practical setup: recruitment, tooling, consent, and timeline.

## Research Brief Inputs
- Research objective
- Business objective
- Target users
- Method choice
- Decision deadline

## Recruitment Basics
Define:
- Inclusion criteria
- Exclusion criteria
- Segment quotas
- Incentive policy

## Sample Guidelines (Rule of Thumb)
- Qual interviews: 5–15
- Usability tests: 5–8 (early rounds)
- Surveys: 100+
- Diary studies: 8–15

## Screener Checklist
- Behavior-based criteria
- Avoid revealing desired answers
- Include at least one open response

## Logistics Checklist
- Session script
- Consent language
- Recording setup
- Note-taking owner
- Debrief template

## Data Handling
- Store data in controlled location
- Limit access scope
- Remove personally identifiable information where possible

## Timeline Template (Fast Cycle)
- Day 1: finalize brief + screener
- Day 2–3: recruit + schedule
- Day 4–5: run sessions
- Day 6: synthesis
- Day 7: decision brief

## Output Contract
- Recruit log
- Session log
- Evidence repository
- Decision-ready summary
